Privacy Policy

FluraStock · Last updated: 2026-07-08

FluraStock ("we", "us", "our") operates the website flurastock.github.io and produces AI-generated stock-market research summaries as short videos, which we publish to social platforms including TikTok and YouTube. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

1. Information we access

When the account owner connects a social account (e.g. TikTok via its Content Posting API, or YouTube), we request only the permissions needed to publish our own generated videos:

  • TikTok video.upload — to upload our generated videos to the connected creator's own TikTok inbox/drafts for the creator to review and publish.
  • Basic account identifiers (e.g. open id) returned during authorization, used only to associate the stored token with the connected account.

We do not collect, read, or store personal data of any other TikTok users, nor browse or scrape user content.

2. How we use it

Access tokens are used solely to upload/publish the market-research videos we generate to the account you explicitly authorized. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or profiling.

3. Storage & security

OAuth tokens are stored on a private server with restricted file permissions and are never committed to public code repositories. They are used only for the automated publishing described above.

4. Third parties

Publishing is performed through TikTok and YouTube (Google) APIs; your use of those platforms is also governed by their respective privacy policies. We do not send your data to any other third party.

5. Your control

You may revoke our access at any time from your TikTok settings (Manage app permissions) or Google Account security settings; once revoked, we can no longer publish on your behalf. To request deletion of stored tokens, contact us below.

6. Content disclaimer

All content produced by FluraStock is AI-generated market research for informational purposes only and does not constitute investment advice.

7. Contact

Questions or data requests: flura@mewtant.io
